Breakfast - Raspberry Coconut Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 350ml Coconut Water.
  • 125g Coconut Yoghurt.
  • 40g Protein Powder.
  • 1 Banana.
  • 100g Raspberries.

Instructions:

  • Blend all the ingredients together.

Lunch - Greek Bean Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cucumber /chopped
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper /diced
  • 1/4 Red Onion /diced
  • 14oz can of Cannelini Beans
  • 30g of Olives
  • 75g Feta /crumbled
  • 75g Cherry Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • 1/2tsp Oregano
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions:

  • Mix oil, oregano, salt and pepper.
  • Add tomatoes, pepper, cucumber, onion and beans and mix well.
  • Add to serving bowl and top with olives and feta.

Dinner - Baked Salmon with Kale

Ingredients:

  • 14oz Kale
  • Rosemary
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 225g Salmon Fillets
  • 200g Microwave Brown Rice

Instructions:

  • Pre-heat oven at 320F
  • Place salmon on a sheet of tin foil on a baking tray, top with lemon juice.
  • Add kale, salt and pepper. Seal the foil and then bake for 15-20minutes.
  • Serve salmon and kale over the rice and drizzle any remaining juice and serve.
