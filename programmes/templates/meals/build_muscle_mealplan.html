Breakfast

PBJ Oats

Ingredients:

  • 250ml Milk of your choice.
  • 1 Banana /chopped
  • 20g Peanut Butter
  • 1tsp Jam
  • 50g Oats

Instructions:

  • Add oats and milk to saucepan and boil until.
  • Add Peanut Butter and simmer.
  • Add to Serving bowl and top with banana and jam.

Lunch

Greek Bean Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cucumber /chopped
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper /diced
  • 1/4 Red Onion /diced
  • 14oz can of Cannelini Beans
  • 30g of Olives
  • 75g Feta /crumbled
  • 75g Cherry Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • 1/2tsp Oregano
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions:

  • Mix oil, oregano, salt and pepper.
  • Add tomatoes, pepper, cucumber, onion and beans and mix well.
  • Add to serving bowl and top with olives and feta.

Dinner

Lamb Roast Chops

Ingredients:

  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 200g Lamb Chop
  • 600g Potatoes
  • 1 Zucchini
  • 100g Cherry Tomatoes

Instructions:

  • Pre-heat oven at 390F
  • Mix together oil, rosemary, garlic, lemon, salt and pepper. Add lamb, potatoes and zucchini and toss to coat. Add to baking tray and roast for 20minutes.
  • Place tomatoes on the tray and roast for another 5 minutes.
  • Remove from the oven and serve.
